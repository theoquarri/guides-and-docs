Onboarding Guide

Connect Quarri to Claude Desktop

For Claude Team and Enterprise plans. Follow the steps below to add Quarri as a custom connector and start querying your data through Claude.

Admin Setup
Preliminary Steps for Owners
Before members of Team and Enterprise plans can use Quarri, an Owner or Primary Owner needs to add it as a custom connector to the organization.
1
Download Claude Desktop
If you haven't already, download and install the Claude desktop app from claude.com/download.
2
Navigate to Connectors
Open Claude Desktop and go to Customize > Connectors.
3
Add Custom Connector
Press the + button at the top, then select Add custom connector.
4
Enter Connector Details
Fill in the connector details and press Add:
Name: Quarri
Remote MCP server URL: https://app.quarri.ai/mcp
5
Connect to Quarri
Quarri will now appear in your connectors list. Click Connect to authenticate.
6
Log In
You'll be redirected to the Quarri login page in your browser. Log in with your credentials to complete the connection.

Need credentials?

Credentials are generated by the Quarri team or your company's admin. If you don't have credentials, please request them from a Quarri team member or an internal admin.

7
Configure Tool Permissions
Go to Settings > Connectors, click on Quarri, and configure the tool permissions: set Read-only tools to Always allow and Write/delete tools to Needs approval. This ensures Claude calls the correct tools and operates most efficiently.
User Setup
Steps for Team Members
Once an Owner has added the Quarri connector to your organization, follow these steps to connect.
1
Download Claude Desktop
If you haven't already, download and install the Claude desktop app from claude.com/download.
2
Navigate to Connectors
Open Claude Desktop and go to Customize > Connectors. Find the Quarri connector in the list — it will have a "Custom" label. Click Connect to authenticate.
3
Log In
You'll be redirected to the Quarri login page in your browser. Log in with your credentials to complete the connection.

Need credentials?

Credentials are generated by the Quarri team or your company's admin. If you don't have credentials, please request them from a Quarri team member or an internal admin.

4
Configure Tool Permissions
Go to Settings > Connectors, click on Quarri, and configure the tool permissions: set Read-only tools to Always allow and Write/delete tools to Needs approval. This ensures Claude calls the correct tools and operates most efficiently.

Need Help?

Reach out to the Quarri team for setup assistance or credential requests.
